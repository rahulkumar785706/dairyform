MORE THAN
NATURAL
It's not just about producing good quality natural milk from cows. It's about their happiness level. If cows are happy, they produce far better quality milk. Apart from natural fodder and cattle feed we provide, our cows are provided with good housing and space to move around and graze. Their hygiene and health are also taken care of very well by experienced farmers.
FOREST
GRAZED
When cows walk around and graze naturally in the open field, they are very healthy. Our Desi Cows are grazed in the forest during the day. They graze natural grass, leaves, and medicinal herbs. Grazing cows in the open field under the sun not only keep cows healthy, but they also give us nutrition-rich milk. Consuming milk and milk products from forest grazed cows is known to have various health benefits.

OUR
PROCESS

GRAZING

NATURALLY

Our cows are grazed naturally. We feed our cows with naturally grown grass and chemical-free cattle feed along with oil cakes and natural mineral supplements.

CARING

WITH LOVE

Cows in our farms are treated with love and care. We monitor closely to make sure they are properly housed, washed, and health is periodically checked.

MILKING

HYGEINICALLY

Cows are washed regularly and their hygiene is maintained very well. Before milking udder area is thoroughly washed and dried.

CHILLING

FASTER

After milking, milk is chilled as quickly as possible to 4°C. This helps to avoid bacterial growth and maintain freshness.

DELIVERY

ON TIME

Fresh milk is delivered to customers every day before 7 am to their doorsteps. We maintain a strict cold chain end-to-end to maintain freshness.
